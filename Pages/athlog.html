Athlog

Project Overview

Due to the rapid expansion of the fitness industry, there are now a vast amount of fitness apps and products available for people to use. While there are many different apps and websites that can track cardio, there aren't many that can track exercises and have a clean and modern UI. My goal for was to design a new fitness tracker that is easy for beginners to use.

The Problem

There aren't many apps available to track workouts, so develop a new workout tracker with a modern, clean and easy to use design.

Challenges

The biggest challenge for designing a fitness product is the wide variety of fitness enthusiast. From Crossfitters to marathon runners, every group will have a prefence on how their product is designed. So the biggest challenge is to design the app so that it can be helpful for the average fitness enthusiast. It'll be difficult to design an app that everyone will find useful.

Research

The Users

In order to discover what features users are interested in, research was done by surveying the types of fitness apps and products people might already be using.

Other Products

Since there are many different kinds fitness products already available, it was easy to research what some of them do well and what can be improved. This information will be used to plan out the features and layout of the app.

Planning the Design

Informational Architecture and Wireframes

The workflow for this design is extremely important since it needs to be simple and easy to use. The app's main goal is to track workouts and will include other features that the users will find useful such as the ability to track weight, runs, view monthly history and connect with friends. A cool and friendly color pallete will help beginners feel welcomed.

Final Design

With the power of Adobee XD, high fidelity prototypes were created and with user feedback a final design was developed.

Create New Workout Routines

Create as many workout routines as you need. Input weight, reps, sets and time for each exercise to track progress.

Track Your Weight Trends

Use the profile section to update your weight and keep track of weight fluctuations.

View Workout History

The calendar helps you keep track of all the workouts that were previously completed.

Complete Goals and Connect With Friends

Level up by earning points for completing goals. Add workout buddies and compete to see who earns the most points every week!

Record Your Runs

Record your runs and view your run history.

Style Guide

A products branding is extrememly important for fitness products, so it was important to spend a decent amount of time plannning it. The illustrations in the welcome back and login screen were used to help portray a more inclusive and welcoming message. The blue color pallete helps give the design a more modern look.

Final Takeaway

One of the biggest improvements that can be made for the app design is conducting more thorough research. This proved to be most difficult part of designing a product. More research and testing can greatly improve a product's design, but it's definitely difficult to get more without actually being able to develop the product and only relying on prototypes.
